Introduction

This is a Quiz Game with several capabilities. First of all you can select if you want to play the game with your own Json Quiz File or to take questions from the 'opentrivia api' which i use. Also you can pick varius choices for your game (amount of questions, category etc). In conclusion, you have the ability to set a timer for your Quiz Game. I hope to enjoy my effort and of course you can send me your observations about bugs which may be appear. My GitHub: Vasilis Lionis
